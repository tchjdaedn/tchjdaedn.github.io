City cloudiness is not only higly seasonly dependent, but highly circumstantially dependent as well. While there are stochastically predictable averages, the cloudiness of any particular city on any particular day (such as the day the cloudiness measurements were taken) have a highl degree of variance. We can see trends of banding, but these are the result of rounding off measurements rather than any natural phenomena. We do see some grouping vertically, due to cities being clustered in geographically proximal regions.
